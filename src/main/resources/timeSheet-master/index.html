Tips:

You can enter times in any of these formats (where HH means Hours and MM means Minutes): HH:MM,HH.MM,HH,MM. Examples: 1:15, 12.35, 7,37.
You can also enter 3 or 4 digit numbers, and they'll be split up into the formats HMM and HHMM. Examples: 230 will become 2:30, and 1012 will become 10:12.
